2100.00 EGP

The Raspberry Pi Compute Module 4 (CM4) is a compact version of the Raspberry Pi 4, designed for embedded applications. It features a quad-core ARM Cortex-A72 processor running at 1.5 GHz, with options for 2GB, 4GB, or 8GB of RAM, and 8GB, 16GB, or 32GB of eMMC storage. The CM4 has HDMI, USB, PCIe, GPIO, and Gigabit Ethernet interfaces, but it requires a carrier board for easy access to these ports. It is ideal for industrial, commercial, and custom projects where space and flexibility are key.
